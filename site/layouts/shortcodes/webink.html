We were tasked with the design and development of a marketing site for the WebINK font rental service.

A list of fonts was provided from a Java service and organized into a list which could be searched or browsed.

Typefaces samples could be customized and changed. Details on each font were plentiful.

I outlined the entire process into a tour page.

We kept a showcase of sites using the WebINK service.

Users kept and maintained a "Type Drawer" to integrate into their sites.
